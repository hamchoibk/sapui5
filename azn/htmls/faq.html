What is mobile banking?

Mobile banking is a mobile application available on your mobile phone. It gives you 24/7 access to your accounts wherever you are in the world, right in the palm of your hand.

What is the difference between mobile banking and internet banking?

Mobile banking provides you with access to the most popular internet banking features. However, for services such as loan redraw, or opening a new savings account, you will still need to access internet banking.

What features of internet banking are available in mobile banking?

With mobile banking, you can:

  • Transfer funds to anyone in
  • pay bills using Pay Bills, including setting up new billers
  • update and maintain your address, phone number and email address details
  • view your available account balances, transactions and detailed transaction information.

Is my phone compatible with mobile banking?

Aprisma PrimeCash mobile banking has been designed to work on iPhone and iPod Touch devices that can access the internet.

Do I need to register for mobile banking?

No. Members who are registered for internet banking can use mobile banking by browsing to the www.aprisma.co.id website.

How do I access mobile banking?

Open the Safari application on your iPhone, go to aprisma.co.id, select ‘Mobile banking’ from the menu and log in using your current internet banking login and access code.

Is mobile banking secure?

Yes. Mobile banking uses 128-bit Secure Sockets Layer (SSL) protocol encryption which is the same security and authentication as internet banking.

What if my phone is lost or stolen?

You do not need to contact Aprisma PrimeCash as your account details and personal information are not stored on your phone. Additionally, you can disable mobile banking access in internet banking by selecting ‘Mobile banking’ from the ‘Other’ menu.

Can I access mobile banking when overseas?

Yes. Mobile roaming charges may apply. Contact your mobile service provider before accessing mobile banking from outside Indonesia.

Can I access my account using mobile banking if my account requires 2 to sign?

No. Mobile banking does not support the functionality to enable 2 to sign access. These accounts can only be accessed via internet banking.

Is mobile banking free?

Yes. Accessing Aprisma PrimeCash mobile banking is free. However, standard mobile phone contract and mobile service charges will apply.

How do I transfer and make payments using mobile banking?

Mobile banking offers two options for making payments, transfers and PayBills. To transfer to a Aprisma PrimeCash account or another financial institution within Indonesia select the ‘Transfer’ option. You can select your Aprisma PrimeCash accounts, existing personal payees or pay a new recipient. If you’re paying a bill using a BPAY biller code and reference number, select the ‘BPAY’ option. You can select existing personal payees or pay a new biller.

How do I add or modify existing payee or biller details?

To add a new payee or biller select ‘Pay new’ in the ‘Transfer to’ section of mobile banking. To delete or make changes to an existing payee or biller you will need to log into internet banking.

Can I make future dated payments?

Future dated payments can be made once only or as a recurring payment. Select either ‘Once on’ or ‘Recurring’ from the ‘Transfer Money’ or ‘Pay Bill’ pages in mobile banking.

How do I modify a recurring or future dated payment?

To delete or modify a recurring or future dated payment you will need to log into internet banking.

How do I print receipts using mobile banking?

iPhone and iPod Touch devices do not allow printing. To save a screen shot of your payment receipt screen to your Camera Roll, simply press the menu and the on/off buttons simultaneously.

What happens to my mobile banking session when my phone becomes inactive or I switch applications?

For security, your mobile banking session will close after 10 minutes of inactivity. To begin another mobile banking session, simply log in again.

What does the processing screen mean?

The processing screen will display after you make a selection in mobile banking. While this screen is displayed, your request is loading in the background.

Does mobile banking require any specific browser configuration settings?

No, the mobile banking is a native application. It doesn't need any specific settings related to local browser.
